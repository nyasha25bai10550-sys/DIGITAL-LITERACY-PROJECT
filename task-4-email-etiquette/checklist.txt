Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist

 Do's
- Do maintain a professional profile picture and bio.
- Do think before posting content online.
- Do respect others’ opinions and engage politely.
- Do fact-check information before sharing.
- Do use privacy settings to control who sees your posts.

 Don'ts
- Don't post offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content.
- Don't share personal information publicly.
- Don't engage in online arguments or cyberbullying.
- Don't plagiarize or share someone else's work without credit.
- Don't spend excessive time on social media during study hours.
