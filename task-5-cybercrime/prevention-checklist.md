Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist
	1.	Verify sender details before clicking links in emails or messages.
	2.	Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank.
	3.	Check website URLs for authenticity (look for https and correct domain names).
	4.	Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.
	5.	Use strong, unique passwords for all online accounts.
	6.	Avoid public Wi-Fi for online banking or shopping.
	7.	Monitor bank/UPI transactions daily and report suspicious activity immediately.
	8.	Report phishing or fraud to the [National Cyber Crime Portal] (https://cybercrime.gov.in) or call Helpline 1930.
